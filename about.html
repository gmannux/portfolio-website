My Story

"I was raised in "The Golden State" of California and ventured out east to "The Big Apple" after completing my undergraduate degree. During my time in New York, I worked as a successful real estate agent in downtown Manhattan.

While working as an agent I always had a keen interest in marketing and web design. My curiosity for design led me to Career Foundry where I completed a UI/UX design immersion course. As a primarily self-taught designer, I am constantly seeking new challenges to create the most unique user-friendly interfaces.

My previous real estate background with building client relationships, studying markets, and delivering results has been valuable in my transition to a UI/UX designer. I pride myself on having a keen eye for visual design that has aided me in creating aesthetically balanced user-interfaces & cohesive brand identities for my products. I hope to further my education in UI/UX design by pursuing a Masters in Human-Computer Interaction at UC Irvine beginning in Fall 2021.

I am a great believer in Feng Shui and having harmony & balance in our designs and lives. In my downtime, I can be found traveling the world or cooking a full course meal in my kitchen for family & friends. Want to learn more? Let's connect! I would love to chat about new opportunities or anything design related. Please reach out to me at gkmannx@gmail.com."
